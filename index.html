    Short Film Making Rules

    ×

    Registration Fees : Nill
    DURATION:
    ● The minimum length should be 30 seconds excluding credits.
    ● The maximum length shall not be more than Three minutes (180 seconds), including the beginning and end credits.
    ● Films not adhering to the time limit are liable to be rejected.
    ● Opening and/or closing credits are completely optional.

    LANGUAGE:
    ● Films may be in any language or have no dialogue at all.
    ● Films submitted previously to any other festival will not be considered for this contest. If found later, film will be disqualified for the contest/prize withheld.

    RIGHT TO SCREEN:
    ● The participants grant the Organisers, the non-exclusive rights to screen (non –commercial) the film on their respective websites, at the festival and other associated screenings.

    COPYRIGHT:
    ● The Film must not contain any copyrighted work(s) belonging to third parties unless (1) Participants have a license to use such works in the Film; or (2) Participants’ Film makes use of such works in a fair manner under the copyright laws..
    ● Participants will retain any licenses and provide them to organisers upon request.
    ● Each participant must bring college I'd and show at time of registration.
    ● ●Participant must be fully vaccinated.

    *T&C : Any kind of cheating will lead to disqualification.

    Coding Challenge Rules

    ×

    Registration Fees : Nil

    • The Event will be held completely in Offline Mode. Systems with high internet speed will be provided to the participants who will attend the event in college premises.
    • There is no registration fees.
    • BVICAM Code Challenge consists of 2 rounds.


      • ROUND 1

    • Round 1 will be an elimination round.
    • It consists of a quiz that will test your knowledge in Computer Science.
    • It will have 30 questions, and the time limit will be 20 minutes.
    • Prelims will be start at 9:30am and will be open till 12:30pm. Participant can give their test at any time between this But you will get only one shot. Make sure you hit bullseye.
    • Results will be announced at 2pm.


      • ROUND 2

    • Those participants, who cleared Round 1 will be eligible to attempt the Round 2.
    • Round 2 will be a coding round .
    • It will commence at 3 pm sharp.
    • There will be 3 coding questions and you will be given 2 hours.
    • You will be allowed to use any programming language for solving these questions.
    • Participants will be judged on the basis of submission time and complexities of their solutions

    Radio Jockey Rules

    ×

    Event Brief: This event gives you the chance to own your show with live audience right by your side. Be a Radio Jockey! Show off your voice and communication skills. Speak a lot and you have chance to win!

    Event Rules:
    ● Participants will be judged on the basis of one round.
    ● Participants need to speak for minimum two to maximum five minutes.
    ● Exceeding time limit will result in deduction of marks.
    ● Topic has to be of event related to national or international importance.
    ● Participants will be judged on the basis of fluency, spontaneity, presentation & communication skills.
    ● Language of the script should be suitable for presentation in an academic institution.
    ● Participants will have to present in front of physical audience within the campus.
    ● Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    ● Each participant must bring college I'd and show at time of registration.
    ● Participant must be fully vaccinated.
